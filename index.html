Welcome to

The Immortal Karuta

The story begins like this....

While working late one night on a report on a certain English poet,
playwright, and actor, a figure who is widely regarded as the greatest
writer in the English language and the world's greatest dramatist.
You feel a malaise come over you.

You blink as you struggle to keep your eyes open. It was then, on
the third blink, you found yourself transported into a dark grimey dungeon.
You try to find a way out but the corridors are never ending.

It was then you notice the writing on the wall

To escape my labyrinth you have
to pass a series of tests.

Match the prose written on the walls with a
corresponding engraving and I might just let you go
fail me 3 times and you will never leave

You have lost my patience, you shall wander these dungeons forever!
